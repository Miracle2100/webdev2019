Powerful PR: Narrative Influence on Social Change

Every brand has a story to tell and a story to sell.

Understanding how major nonprofit organizations use narrative stragies to influence social change by humanizing complex policy issues is an art and science that corporate institutions must learn to emulate in order to bolster trust and consumer confidence. We'll explore these concepts through the lens of the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention's Tips from Former Smokers campaign and the American Lung Association's Healthy Air campaign.
